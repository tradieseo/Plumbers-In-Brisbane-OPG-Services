Hot Water System Red Flags to Watch


✓ Unstable hot water temperature
✓ The system makes banging, popping, or hissing noises
✓ Leaks or water pooling around the tank or pipes
✓ Hot water is slow to come out or has low pressure
✓ Water looks rusty or has an unusual smell

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my gas hot water system serviced?
It’s recommended to have a professional inspection at least once a year to ensure safety, efficiency, and prevent unexpected breakdowns.
What type of gas hot water system is best for my home?
It depends on your household size, water usage, and space. Options include continuous flow (instant) or storage tank systems, and a professional can help determine the best fit.
Can a gas hot water system be repaired if it’s over 10 years old?
Yes, many older systems can be repaired, but sometimes replacement is more cost-effective and energy-efficient. Our team will advise you on the best option.
How long does a typical gas hot water system last?
On average, a well-maintained system lasts 10–15 years, but regular servicing can extend its life.

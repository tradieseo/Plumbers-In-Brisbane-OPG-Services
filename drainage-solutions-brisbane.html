Signs of an Outdoor Drain Problem


✓ Strange gurgling or bubbling noises
✓ Slow-draining sinks, baths, or showers
✓ Water backing up through drains
✓ Excess surface water around your property
✓ Wet or soggy patches in your garden
✓ Persistent foul smells
✓ Grease and fat build-up
✓ Tree root intrusion


If you notice any of these warning signs, call our team on for fast, reliable drainage repairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I tell where a drainage problem is coming from?
Stormwater issues usually appear during rain. Sewer issues tend to affect indoor plumbing regardless of weather.
Are drainage services different for commercial properties?
Yes. Commercial systems are often larger and used more frequently, which changes how issues are assessed and repaired.
Can a drainage problem get worse if left alone?
Yes. Minor drainage issues can spread to other areas and become harder to fix over time.
Do I need to be on site during drainage work?
Not always. Access requirements depend on the location of the drain and the type of work involved.
Do drainage repairs always involve digging?
No. Many repairs can be completed without excavation, depending on pipe condition and access.

Smell Gas? Follow These Safety Steps


✓ Turn off the gas supply at the meter if it’s safe to do so
✓ Open doors and windows to ventilate the area

Our Advanced Gas Leak Detection Methods

As experienced gas leak detection plumbers, we use cutting-edge tools and proven methods to quickly locate even hard-to-find gas leaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if there’s a gas leak in my property?
Signs can include unusual odours, hissing sounds near gas lines, or dead plants around outdoor pipes.
Is it safe to stay inside while waiting for a gas professional?
If the leak is strong, it’s best to evacuate until the area is ventilated and assessed.
Can gas leaks cause health problems?
Yes, exposure may lead to dizziness, headaches, nausea, or in extreme cases, asphyxiation.
How often should gas systems be inspected?
Annual inspections are recommended to ensure pipes, valves, and appliances are in good condition.

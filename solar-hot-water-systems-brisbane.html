The Best Solar Hot Water System For You

Whether it’s for your home or business, our team delivers solar hot water installation in Brisbane with the right system matched to your usage, layout, and budget.


✓ Solar Hot Water Storage Systems - Tank-based systems that store hot water ready for daily use in homes and small businesses.
✓ Split System Solar Hot Water Units - Ground-mounted tank with roof collectors, ideal when roof space, weight, or access is limited.
✓ Close-Coupled Solar Hot Water Systems - All-in-one rooftop unit (tank + collectors) for a simple, cost-effective solar setup.
✓ Heat Pump Hot Water Systems - High-efficiency systems that heat water using ambient air to reduce power use year-round.


Maintaining Your Solar Hot Water System

Solar hot water systems require very little maintenance, which makes them cost-effective to run. However, servicing helps keep the system efficient and avoids performance drop-offs over time.


Our maintenance includes checking the collectors and pipework, inspecting valves and fittings, testing pressure and temperature, and confirming the system is heating properly. If anything is worn or starting to fail, we’ll flag it early.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I install solar hot water if my roof gets some shade?
Yes, but heavy shade can reduce performance. In shaded homes, a split system or heat pump may be a better option.
Can I replace my old system with a different brand?
Yes. You can switch brands, but the system should be matched to your home’s setup and pipework to avoid performance issues.
Is solar hot water cheaper to run than electric hot water?
In most cases, yes. Solar reduces the amount of electricity needed to heat water, especially in sunny conditions.

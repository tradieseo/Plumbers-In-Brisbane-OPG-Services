Our Plumbing Repairs

We respond quickly to problems around your home, fixing issues safely and efficiently so life can get back to normal.


✓ Tap and Fixture Repairs - Covers leaking taps, dripping mixers, worn washers or cartridges, loose handles, low pressure at fixtures, and faulty diverter valves.
✓ Toilet Repairs - Includes running toilets, weak flushing, leaking cisterns, faulty inlet or outlet valves, toilet pan sealing issues, and blocked toilets.
✓ Sink and Basin Repairs - Handles slow or blocked sinks, leaking traps, cracked or loose waste pipes, faulty under-sink plumbing, and unpleasant drain smells.
✓ Shower and Bath Repairs - Covers dripping shower fittings, shower leaks behind walls, low pressure, faulty shower diverters, leaking bath wastes, overflow leaks, and loose bathroom tapware.
✓ Appliance Plumbing Repairs - Includes leaking or worn flexi hoses, dishwasher connection issues, washing machine hose leaks, and loose isolation valves.
✓ Internal Leak Detection and Minor Pipe Repairs - Addresses small internal leaks, corroded flexi hoses, leaking joints, water hammer in older homes, and issues with internal stop taps.
